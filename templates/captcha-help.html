How to read a clock

On this human verification sample the shorter red hand marks the hours and the longer blue hand marks the minutes.
The correct answer here is 09:23.

The hour hand is over 09 but not yet at 10, therefore the hours are 09.

The minute hand is over 20 but not yet at 25, therefore the minutes at that position are 23.
There are four positions between the 20 and 25. The previous position of the hand would be 22 and the next one is 24.

Setting AM or PM does not matter. Both work with the correct time. Good luck!
